  • What is Herbarium?
    Herbarium is a natural beauty and wellness brand dedicated to creating plant-based skincare, haircare, and body products. We combine traditional herbal wisdom with modern formulations to promote healthy, radiant beauty — naturally..
  • Are your products 100% natural?
    Yes. All our products are made using pure herbal extracts, essential oils, and natural ingredients — free from harmful chemicals, parabens, sulfates, or artificial fragrances.
  • Are your products safe for sensitive skin?
    Absolutely. Our products are dermatologically tested and gentle on all skin types. However, since everyone’s skin is unique, we recommend doing a small patch test before first use..
  • How should I store your products?
    Keep products in a cool, dry place, away from direct sunlight. Since our formulations are natural and preservative-free, store them tightly sealed to maintain freshness.
  • Produce Your Own Clean Save The Environment
    Reduce, reuse, and recycle: This is a classic but effective way to reduce waste and conserve resources. Try to use reusable bags, containers, and water bottles, and recycle items that can't be reused.
  • Do you deliver or ship products?
    Yes, we offer nationwide delivery and work with reliable courier services to ensure your order arrives safely and on time.
  • Where do you source your ingredients?
    We source our herbs and botanicals from trusted local farmers and sustainable growers to ensure the highest quality and freshness in every product.
